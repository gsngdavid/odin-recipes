Chicken masala

Want to try homemade Masala Chicken? Follow this easy chicken masala recipe with step by step!

Ingredients

  • 750 gm chicken
  • 2 cup chopped onion
  • salt as required
  • 2 inches cinnamon stick
  • 2 green cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala powder
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 4 tablespoon mustard oil
  • 1/2 cup minced tomato
  • 3 teaspoon coriander leaves
  • 2 bay leaf
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon powdered turmeric
  • 2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 2 green chillies
  • 2 teaspoon kasoori methi powder

Steps

  1. Wash and clean the chicken
  2. Saute whole spices
  3. Add spices in frying chicken
  4. Cook for 2-4 minutes with closed lid and serve hot
